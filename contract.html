SWIFT RENTALS LLC – VEHICLE USE AGREEMENT

This Vehicle Use Agreement is entered into between Swift Rentals LLC and the undersigned authorized individual. By signing below, the Driver agrees to all terms and conditions herein.

1. Vehicle Authorization & Purpose

The Driver is authorized to use the vehicle specified by Swift Rentals LLC to carry out their own business or personal activities, without restriction on mileage or the nature of such activities.

2. Vehicle Condition

The Driver has thoroughly inspected the vehicle prior to rental and confirms that no damage exists, except as specifically noted by Swift Rentals LLC.

3. Fuel & Charging Requirements

Gas vehicles must be returned with the same fuel level as pickup. If not, Swift Rentals LLC may charge $6.00 per gallon. Electric vehicles may be charged $0.07 per mile driven to cover battery recharging costs.

4. Tolls & Citations

Any tolls incurred during the rental period will be rounded up to the next whole dollar and charged against the Driver’s deposit. Traffic citations and parking tickets are the sole responsibility of the Driver.

5. Smoking Fee

A non-refundable cleaning fee of $125.00 will be charged if smoking occurs inside the rental vehicle.

6. Damage & Liability

If the vehicle is returned with damage, the Driver may forfeit their deposit. The Driver is financially responsible for damages, repair costs, diminished value, towing, storage, and related losses if at fault.

7. Key / Key Fob Replacement

If the vehicle keys or key fob are lost, the Driver authorizes Swift Rentals LLC to charge $250.00.

8. Return Date & Late Policy

The Driver agrees to return the vehicle on or before the scheduled return date and time. Failure to return or extend the rental may result in the vehicle being reported stolen, legal action, and forfeiture of deposit.

9. Payment Authorization & Refunds

The Driver authorizes Swift Rentals LLC to process the rental amount, applicable fees, and deposit charges using the payment method provided. Deposit refunds may take 7–10 business days.

10. Non-Dispute Agreement

The Driver acknowledges and agrees that by accepting these terms, they waive the right to dispute charges processed under this Agreement, which is considered legally binding.
